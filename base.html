About Me

The Tutoring Process

My tutoring process involves assessing each student's unique strengths and areas for improvement. I begin with an evaluation to understand their current level of mastery in mathematics and writing. From there, I create a structured plan that includes targeted exercises, practice problems, and writing assignments. I encourage open communication and regular feedback so that students can express what they find challenging. This collaborative approach not only helps them improve academically but also nurtures a love for learning.
